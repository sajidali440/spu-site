What makes this interface unique for SPU?

Instead of separate pages for Home, Academics, Notices and Contact, everything is pulled into one holo window. The left dock behaves like a small OS launcher and the content transitions smoothly within the console. The design stays light on the eyes with a dark default theme and a bright alt theme for daytime viewing.
