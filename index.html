What makes this interface unique for SPU?

Instead of separate pages for Home, Academics, Notices and Contact, everything is pulled into one holo window. The left dock behaves like an OS launcher and the content transitions smoothly within the console. The design stays light on the eyes with a dark neon theme and a clean academic light mode for daytime viewing.
